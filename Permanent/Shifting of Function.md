# Shifting of Function
==2024-11-14

---
> Shifting of $y=f(x)$
---
## Theory
> [!important] 
> $y=f(x)+a$ -> $\text{if } a>0$ shifts up by a units, else down by |a| units. 
> $y=f(x-a)$ -> $\text{if } a>0$ shifts right by a units, else left by |a| units. 

---
## Examples
For $y=f(x)=x^2$, shiftings:

> [!example] $y=f(x)+a, a > 0$
> $y=x^2+2$ -> a>0 : green
> $y=x^2-2$->a<0     : purple
> $y=(x-2)^2$->a>0   : black
> $y=(x+2)^2$->a<0   : red

>> ```
