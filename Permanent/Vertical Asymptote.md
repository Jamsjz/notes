# Vertical Asymptote
==2024-11-22

---
## Theory
$y=f(x)$ has a vertical [[Asymptote]] at x=a if,
$\lim_{ x \to a^- }=\pm\infty$,
$\lim_{ x \to a^+ }=\pm\infty$
---
## Examples
```desmos-graph
y=(x^2-1)/x
x=0
```
$f(x)=\frac{x^2-1}{x}$
Here,
At $x\to 0^+$, $y\to -\infty$ , $x\to 0^-$, $y\to +\infty$
So, $x=0$ is the vertical asymptote of $f(x)$.
