## Theory
Transpose of [[Conjugate of a Matrix]] is the
Tranjugate of a matrix and is denoted by,
$A^*$.

### Hermitian Matrix
If $A^*=A$, then it is a Hermitian Matrix.

> [!important] 
>Symmetric Matrix is a special case of
>hermitian matrix.
