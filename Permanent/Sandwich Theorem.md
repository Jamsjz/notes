# Sandwich Theorem
==2024-11-15

---
## Theory
Given,
$g(x)\leq f(x)\leq h(x)$ then,
$\lim_{ x \to x_{o} }g(x)=\lim_{ x \to x_{o} }h(x)\implies \lim_{ x \to x_{o} }f(x)$

---
## Examples
$2-x^2\leq g(x)\leq2cos x\implies \lim_{ x \to 0 }g(x)=2$
