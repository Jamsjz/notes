# Complex Matrix
==2024-12-03

---
> Sometimes complex is not so complex.
---
## Theory
A complex matrix $C$ is defined as:
$C=[a_{jk}]_{m\times n}$, $a_{jk}=c+id\in \mathbb{C}$, $c,d\in \mathbb{R}$
where,
$i$ is the imaginary unit,
$j\in[1,m]$, $k\in[1,n]$ 

---

## Examples
$$
\begin{bmatrix}
1+2i&3+4i \\
2&0 
\end{bmatrix}
$$
