==2024-12-03

## Theory
Transpose of a [[Matrix]] $A=[a_{ij}]_{m\times n}$, is defined as:
$$
A^T=[a_{ji}]_{n\times m}
$$
where,
$1\leq j\leq m$
$1\leq i\leq n$

---
### Properties of Transpose
1. $(A^{T})^{T}=A$
2. $(AB)^T=B^TA^T$
3. $(rA+sB)^T=rA^T+sB^T$ where $r,s\in \mathbb{R}$

---
### Symmetric Matrix
$A=A^T$
---
### Skew-symmetric Matrix
$A=-A^T$
