## Theory
A sequence is denoted by, 
$$
A=\{a_{n}\}={a_{1},a_{2},a_{3},\dots,a_{n}}
$$

A series is denoted by, 
$$
S_{n}=\sum_{k=1}^{n} a_{k}
$$
