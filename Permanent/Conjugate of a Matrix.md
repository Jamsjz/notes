## Theory
For a [[Complex Matrix]] $C=[a_{jk}]_{m\times n}$, $a_{ij}=c+id\in \mathbb{C}$, $c,d\in \mathbb{R}$,
It's Conjugate is defined as:
$$
\bar{C}=[\bar{a_{jk}}]_{m\times n}
$$
