# Properties of Matrix Operations
==2024-12-03

---
> You are good, so are your properties :LiSmile:.

## Theory
1.  $A+B=B+A$
2.  $(A+B)+C=A+(B+C)$
3.  $A+0=A$
4.  $r(A+B)=rA+rB$
5.  $A(BC)=(AB)C$
6.  $A(B+C)=AB+AC$
7.  $(B+C)A=BA+CA$
8.  $r(AB)=(rA)B=A(rB)$
9.  $I_{m}A=AI_{n}$= $A_{m\times n}$
10. $(A^T)^T=A$
11. $(I_{n})^T=I_{n}$
12. $AA^{-1}=A^{-1}A=I_{n}$
13. $(AB)^{-1}=B^{-1}A^{-1}$
14. $(rA)^{-1}=B^{-1}A^{-1}$ iff $B=rI$ or B is a diagonal matrix
15. $(I_{n})^{-1}=I_{n}$
16. $(A^T)^{-1}=(A^{-1})^{T}$
17. $(A^{-1})^{-1}=A$
18. $(\bar{A})^T=\bar{(A^T)}$
19. $\bar{\bar{A}}=A$
20. $(AB)^T=B^TA^T$
